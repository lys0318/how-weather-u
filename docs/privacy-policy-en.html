How Weather You ("the App") treats your personal data with care and complies with applicable laws, including the Korean Personal Information Protection Act. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Personal Data We Collect

CategoryItemsMethod
Account Email address, name, profile photo URL (on Google sign-in) Google OAuth
Location Current latitude/longitude (used once at weather lookup, not stored) OS location permission
Usage records Daily request count (for rate limiting), request timestamps Collected automatically by the App
Local data Generated messages, bookmarks (stored only on your device) Saved automatically by the App
Profile (optional) Nickname, age range, occupation, interests, current concerns (on Google sign-in, for message personalization · entirely optional) Entered in the App

2. Purpose of Use

  • User identification and keeping you signed in
  • Looking up weather based on your current location
  • Providing weather-based messages, activity/food suggestions, and fortunes
  • Counting usage to apply a daily request limit (abuse-prevention cap)
  • Sending notifications
  • Showing ads, including personalized ads (Google AdMob)
  • Service usage statistics and quality improvement

4. Retention and Use Period

  • Account info: Until you delete your account. Tapping [Delete account] in the App deletes it permanently and immediately.
  • Usage records (usage_log): Kept to apply the daily request limit; deleted immediately together with your account when you delete it.
  • Profile (optional): Kept only if you filled it in; deleted immediately with your account on deletion. You can edit or clear it anytime from the profile screen in the App.
  • Location: Not stored (used once at request time, then immediately discarded).
  • Local message history: Kept on your device for 7 days, then auto-deleted (bookmarked messages are kept indefinitely). Deleting your account also removes data stored on your phone.

5. Advertising

The App shows ads through Google AdMob in order to provide the service for free.

  • AdMob may collect and use your Advertising ID (AAID) and device info to deliver personalized ads.
  • For details on Google's ad data processing, see Google Ads Policy and Google Privacy Policy.
  • Opting out of personalized ads: On your phone, go to [Settings → Google → Ads] to "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization."
  • The App is not directed to children under 14 and does not serve child-directed ads.

6. Your Rights

You may exercise the following rights at any time.

  • Access, correction, deletion: View and partially change data directly in the App's settings.
  • Account deletion and removal of all data: See "How to Delete Your Account" below.
  • Withdrawing consent: Location/notification permissions can be revoked anytime at [Settings → Apps → How Weather You] on your phone.

How to Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account and all related data directly in the App, immediately.

  • Open the App → bottom [Settings] tab → tap [Delete account] at the bottom.
  • After two confirmations it is permanently deleted immediately (within seconds).
  • What is deleted: account (email/name), usage records on the server (usage_log), on-device data (messages/bookmarks/settings).
  • You may sign up again with the same Google account afterward, but previous data cannot be recovered.
  • If you cannot use the App for some reason, email tosca0318@gmail.com and we will process it within 7 days.

7. Security Measures

  • All communication is encrypted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+).
  • Sensitive data such as API keys is used only on the backend (Supabase Edge Functions) and never exposed in the client app.
  • User data is accessible only to its owner via Supabase Row Level Security (RLS).
  • Administrative access is limited to a minimal number of people and is logged.

8. Children Under 14

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 14. If such data is found to have been collected, it is deleted immediately.
